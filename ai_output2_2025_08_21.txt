*   Intense cross-party negotiations are underway to form a new government after the ANC lost its parliamentary majority in the recent elections.
*   The African National Congress (ANC) is actively proposing a "Government of National Unity" (GNU) model to include multiple political parties.
*   Key parties, including the Democratic Alliance (DA), Economic Freedom Fighters (EFF), and uMkhonto weSizwe (MK) Party, are engaged in ongoing discussions regarding potential coalitions or participation in a GNU.
*   The first sitting of the 7th Parliament, where the President and Speaker will be elected, is expected to take place soon, potentially by the end of the week, amidst these critical political talks.
